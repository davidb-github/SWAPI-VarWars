EPISODE XLIV
The Fetch Awakens


It is a time of unrest. A computer virus has infected the galaxy, crippling trade, and forcing civilizations to fight over basic necessities like toilet paper. A young coder named Ima Noobie detected a flaw in the malicious software and has devised a plot to restore trade and bring peace to the galaxy.


Young Noobie discovered the source code originated on a moon-sized space station called The Breath Star, capable of transmitting the virus to systems up to six light years away. The code was written by a ruthless hacker named Java the Script in an attempt to extort and enslave billions.


Ima has assembled a team of leading pilots, developers, and software epidemiologists to race towards The Breath Star in hopes of covering it with a giant mask, thereby blocking the space station's two main exhaust ports and further preventing the spread of the malicious virus. Once the station is incapacitated, the team will attempt to refactor the evil code to send a patch out to all infected systems in effort to restore peace.
